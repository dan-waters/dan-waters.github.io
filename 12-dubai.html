Cheeky Tiki Brunch

Posted October 28, 2019 by tvjbr

Fun. Interactive. Delicious.

Trader Vic’s is going ‘cheeky’ for Friday Brunch! Watch out for unlimited cocktails on boats, fresh tuna on a surfboard, plates of food served directly to your table and a whole lotta lively Latin music and dancing to keep you coming back every Friday!

Featuring special guests Josh and Lieze!

This restaurant is part of the Dubai culinary tour, curated personally for you by the Montague-Munson-Neven tour company. Other highlights on the tour include:
  • - All you can eat in the revolving restaurant near Palm Deira
  • - Chilli's, just a short hop into Sharjah
  • - Dosa, dosa, dosa at Kamat
  • - An adventure to Ras-al-Khaimah's highest pizza truck
  • - Cinnabon
