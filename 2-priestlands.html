About Joshua Montague-Munson

Welcome from the Headteacher

Welcome to the Priestlands School website.

Joshua had a wonderful time at Priestlands. We all enjoyed the school discos and the seemingly unregulated lunchtimes. Not all of us got chauffeured to school in a taxi though, or accidentally dyed our hair blonde.

Luckily most of the staff at the school were kind, and called Joshua names like 'Laser'. Who could forget rugby in the bottom PE set with Mr Rodway? Or Mr Bunce? Or Ms Strath?

But which iconic Geography teacher played the recorder and collected hundreds of soil samples?

a) Mr Rowe b) Mr Coller c) Ms McDonald
